Strawberry Milkshake

Prep Time: 10 mins

Yield: 4 minutes

Ingredients






Steps

  1. Steer together butter and parmesan cheese in a small bowl
  2. Blendhalf of strawberries, 1/4tsp. vanilla, 1 cup ice cream and 2 Tbsp. milk in blender until smooth.
  3. Pour into 2 glasses. Repeating with remaining strawberries, vanilla, ice cream, and milk. Serve immediately.
