Cookie

Prep Time: 10 mins

Yield: 4 minutes

Ingredients






Steps

  1. Steer together butter and parmesan cheese in a small bowl
  2. Spread 1 1/12 tsp. butter on 1 side of each bread slice. Place 4 bread slices, buttered sides down, on wax paper. top with provolone and mozzarella cheeses; top with remaining bread slices, buttered sides up.
  3. Cook sandwiches; in batches, on a hot griddle or in a non-stick skillet over medium heat' gently pressing with spatula, 4 minutes on each side or until golden brown and cheese is smelted.
